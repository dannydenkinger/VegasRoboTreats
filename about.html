HOW IT STARTED

Vegas Robo Treats started with a simple observation: local businesses have unused space, and customers love unique experiences. We saw an opportunity to bring both together.

In 2024, we partnered with Sweet Robo — the world's leading manufacturer of robotic dessert machines — to bring their technology to the Las Vegas Valley. The concept was straightforward: place cutting-edge machines in local businesses at zero cost, handle all the operations, and share the revenue. The business owner gets a new income stream. The customers get an unforgettable experience. And we get to do what we love — bringing the future of food to the streets of Vegas.

Today, NextGen Provisions LLC operates across the entire Las Vegas metropolitan area, serving businesses from the Strip to Henderson, Summerlin to North Las Vegas. Every machine we place is fully insured, professionally maintained, and monitored 24/7. We're not a vending company — we're a partnership company that happens to use robots.
