Free Robotic Vending Machines Across the Las Vegas Valley

Vegas Robo Treats proudly serves local businesses with free robotic vending machines across the greater Las Vegas area. Our cotton candy robots, ice cream machines, and 3D chocolate printers are currently available for placement in restaurants, bars, hotels, retail stores, gyms, salons, barbershops, entertainment venues, and more throughout Clark County, Nevada.
