FAQ

Everything you need to know about partnering with Vegas Robo Treats.

What's the catch? Is it really free? expand_more
There is no catch. We provide, install, and maintain the machine at zero cost to your business. We make our money from product sales — and you earn from every sale just for hosting the machine. It's a true win-win partnership.
How much space does the machine need? expand_more
Our cotton candy machine needs roughly 4.5' x 2.5' of floor space (134 x 69 cm). The ice cream machine is about 3' x 4' (87 x 120 cm). The ChocoPrint 3D chocolate printer needs about 3.7' x 2.1' (112 x 65 cm). All machines run on standard power — no special wiring or plumbing required.
How do I earn, and how does payment work? expand_more
We offer two models — revenue share or fixed rent. With revenue share, you earn a cut of every sale based on terms we agree on together. With fixed rent, you receive a guaranteed flat monthly payment just for hosting. Either way, we pay you monthly. Reach out and we'll find the right fit for your location.
How long is the contract? expand_more
Our standard partnership agreement is 12 months. After that, it renews month-to-month and either party can end the partnership with 30 days' written notice.
Can I cancel if it's not working out? expand_more
Yes. After the initial 12-month term, you can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. We'll remove the machine at no cost. During the initial term, early cancellation is available with a small removal fee.
Do I or my staff need to do anything? expand_more
Nothing at all. We handle installation, restocking, cleaning, maintenance, and all payment processing. The machine runs completely autonomously. Your staff never needs to touch it.
What about insurance and liability? expand_more
Every machine is covered by our comprehensive liability insurance policy. We also carry workers' comp for our service technicians and comply with all local health and safety regulations.
What types of businesses qualify? expand_more
We partner with a wide range of businesses — restaurants, bars, hotels, retail stores, gyms, salons, entertainment venues, car dealerships, office buildings, and more. If you have foot traffic and floor space, you likely qualify. Apply and we'll do a quick assessment.
