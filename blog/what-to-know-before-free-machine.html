The Deployment Briefing: What Every Las Vegas Venue Operator Should Know

April 19, 2026

TL;DR — The Deployment Briefing

  • ►Your Job: Provide the space and an outlet. That's it. We handle everything else.
  • ►Service: Local team responds same-day. Spare parts on hand. Thorough cleaning and inspection on every visit.
  • ►Revenue: We offer two models — revenue share or fixed monthly rent — terms agreed per location. You earn from day one.

There is no catch. A free robotic dessert machine placement from Vegas Robo Treats is exactly what it sounds like: we provide the machine, we operate it, we maintain it, we restock it — and you earn a share of every sale generated in your space. Here is exactly how it works from the moment you inquire to the moment your machine is earning.

What You Do: Almost Nothing

This is the part that surprises most operators. Your only responsibilities are to provide a small area of floor space — approximately 4 feet by 4 feet — a standard 120V electrical outlet, and access for our team to service the machine. That is the entire commitment. No inventory. No cleaning. No troubleshooting calls. No staff training. The machine runs completely autonomously, and we take care of every operational detail behind the scenes.

What Vegas Robo Treats Handles

  • ►Delivery & Installation: We bring the machine, set it up, and commission it. No cost, no coordination required from you.
  • ►Restocking: Ingredients are monitored remotely and replenished on a regular schedule. Your machine is always ready to sell.
  • ►Cleaning & Inspection: Every service visit includes a thorough cleaning and full inspection to prevent issues before they occur.
  • ►Maintenance: Our local team keeps spare parts on hand for same-day resolution of any mechanical issue — no waiting on shipping.
  • ►Compliance & Insurance: We handle all permits, health compliance, and carry full liability coverage. Zero exposure to your business.

Same-Day Service. Spare Parts On Hand.

Machine reliability is a core part of our offer — not a footnote. We monitor every machine remotely through telemetry systems that alert our team to issues before they become visible to guests. When service is needed, we respond the same day. We keep a stock of spare components specifically to avoid the delays that come with ordering parts. If something needs to be fixed, it gets fixed fast — because a machine that is not selling is a machine that is not earning, and that is bad for both of us.

Every visit also includes a full cleaning and preventative inspection. This is not just about hygiene — it is about preventing the kind of small issues that turn into downtime. A machine that is consistently clean and serviced performs better, lasts longer, and creates a better impression in your space.

How You Earn

We offer two revenue models — a revenue share based on sales generated at your location, or a fixed monthly rent for the floor space. Exact terms are agreed per location and depend on factors like foot traffic, business type, and placement quality. Either way, you are generating passive income from a Las Vegas robotic vending machine from the moment it goes live. No minimum requirements, no revenue thresholds to hit, no fees of any kind on your side.

The Process: Inquiry to Earning

Getting started is simple. You submit an inquiry through our contact form, and our team reviews your location — business type, estimated foot traffic, and available space. If it is a fit, we schedule a brief site visit, confirm placement, and coordinate delivery. From first inquiry to a live, earning machine typically takes two to three weeks. We also have expanding machine offerings and can discuss custom concepts if you have a specific space or brand experience in mind.

Common Questions, Direct Answers

Is there really no cost to me? None. No purchase price, no lease, no maintenance fee, no revenue minimum. We cover all operational costs and keep the equipment.


What if foot traffic is lower than expected? We evaluate every location before placement. If a machine is underperforming after a fair evaluation period, we work with you on repositioning or adjust the terms. We are here for long-term partnerships, not one-shot placements.


Do I need permits? No. We handle all required permits and compliance as part of our standard process.


Can I choose the machine type? Yes. We work with you to match the right machine to your space and customer base. Browse what is available on our fleet page.
